MOST FEATURE

Machined Aluminum Top-Plate

The top plate is machined from a single block of metal, giving the FUJIFILM X-E5 a more premium feel and smoother dial operation. Its anodized finish further adds a sleek, high-quality appearance.

New Film Simulation Dial

The redesigned Film Simulation dial includes a precision-cut indicator window that greatly reduces the space between the dial and the body for a clean, precise look. You can also assign custom Film Simulation and image quality settings to the FS1-FS3 positions, letting you store your personalized FS recipes.

Mastering the Details

Refinement defines every part of the FUJIFILM X-E5's design. The viewfinder and diopter adjustment sit seamlessly within the body, and the Bluetooth button on the base plate echoes the rewind button found on traditional film cameras.
